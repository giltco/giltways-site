Future models on the platform

These frameworks are shown to establish the wider structure of GILTWays. They indicate where the methodology platform is heading, even though they are not yet launched at the same level of depth as GoxEDGE.

LingoARCH

A technology enablement framework focused on systems, architecture, and operational support for global expansion.

InsureGARO

A governance and risk framework addressing compliance, control, and sustainability requirements in global growth.

How to read this page

Think of this as a platform map, not a promise of simultaneous release.

Not every framework on GILTWays is meant to launch at once. The current phase is intentionally anchored around GoxEDGE. The rest of the library signals structural coherence and future direction, which matters because methodology platforms lose credibility when they look like a random collection of names instead of an ordered system.
